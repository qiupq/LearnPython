Erasing Your Old Computer
An overlooked identity theft risk
Your old clunker of a computer just isn't cutting it anymore. So you bite the bullet, buy a new one, and all your problems are solved. But what to do with Old Faithful? Toss it? Recycle it? Donate it? Give it away?

Whatever you decide, one thing you MUST do is wipe it clean. The last thing you want to do is accidentally leave personal and financial information on the computer for identity thieves to snatch up.

This issue's Security Pod brings you a few simple steps you can take to ensure that when you're done with your computer, your computer is done with you.
